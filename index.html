About Me

I am a software enginering undergraduate at SLTC University, My goal is to balance aesthetics with functionality, ensuring that each design not only looks great but also enhances user satisfaction and ease of use. I start by understanding the user's needs, then craft wireframes and prototypes to map out the user journey.Through careful attention to layout, color, typography, and interaction design, I aim to build interfaces that are both beautiful and seamless to navigate, ultimately delivering a satisfying experience from start to finish.
